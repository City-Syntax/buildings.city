Lightweight UBEM Toolkit V1.0

Build Your City's
Interactive Energy Map

A lightweight toolkit to quickly deploy Urban Building Energy Modeling platforms. Visualize GeoJSON datasets, simulate carbon pathways, and use ML to predict building archetypes locally.

Download the Latest Package Explore Live Demo

3-Step Deployment

  • 1
    Prepare Files: Update config.json and drop your GeoJSON into public/data/.
  • 2
    Archetype Simulation: Connect your simulation-ready energy & carbon intensity values.
  • 3
    Launch: Run npm run dev to serve your interactive 3D urban dashboard.
